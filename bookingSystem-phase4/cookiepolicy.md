# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated:** December 16, 2025

## 1. What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files placed on your device to help the website function properly.

## 2. How We Use Cookies
Our Resource Booking System uses cookies **strictly for technical reasons**. We do not use cookies for advertising, tracking, or marketing purposes.

## 3. The Cookies We Use
We use the following "Strictly Necessary" cookies to ensure the security and functionality of the application:

| Cookie Name | Type | Purpose | Duration |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| `session_id` | Authentication | Identifies your browser session so you remain logged in while navigating between pages. | Session (deleted when browser closes) |
| `csrf_token` | Security | Prevents **Cross-Site Request Forgery (CSRF)** attacks. This ensures that forms you submit really come from you. | Session |

## 4. Managing Cookies
Because these cookies are essential for the core functionality of the website (logging in and submitting bookings securely), they are set by default. You can configure your browser to block these cookies, but if you do so, **you will not be able to log in or make reservations**.
